Readme Instructions for Cabs Online:

1. Open index.html
2. Click Booking button
3. Enter informations that are required and must be entered.
4. Optional informations may be left blank.
5. Click submit booking.
6. Return to index.html
7. Click Admin button
8. Click "Search All Bookings" for searching all available bookings in the system.
9. Click "Search Bookings in 2 Hours" to see bookings within 2 hours later.
10. Enter the Booking Reference Number in the blank space and click "Assign Button" to assign the booking.
